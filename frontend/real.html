LegislationSource: PIB India

President Grants Assent to VB-G RAM G Bill; 125 Days Work Guaranteed

Replacing the 20-year-old MGNREGA, the Viksit Bharat (VB-G RAM G) Act of 2025 has officially been signed into law. The new framework increases the statutory wage employment guarantee from 100 to 125 days per rural household. The Act also introduces a 60:40 funding split between the Centre and States.
