Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify a news forward? +
Navigate to the 'Check' tab in the bottom dock and simply paste the suspicious link or text. Our hybrid verification engine uses advanced AI models alongside human fact-checkers to analyze the content and provide you with an instant credibility verdict.
Is my voting data public? +
Absolutely not. Your voting choices are fully encrypted using military-grade security. We use Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZK-Proof) to verify your eligibility on the blockchain without ever revealing your personal identity or voting history to the public.
What is ONOE? +
'One Nation, One Election' is a constitutional reform initiative aiming to synchronize Lok Sabha and State Assembly elections. The goal is to reduce the frequency of polls, significantly cut down election expenditure, and allow the government to focus on uninterrupted governance.
How to update my address? +
You can request an address change by submitting 'Form 8' directly through the 'Circulars' section of this app. Alternatively, the app provides a secure link to the National Voters' Service Portal (NVSP) where you can upload your proof of residence digitally.
